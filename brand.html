Brand position

Who we are, in a paragraph.

Scale42 is an energy-first developer and operator of next-generation AI data centres across the Nordics. We build the calmest, most credible wholesale infrastructure for hyperscalers, AI labs and HPC operators — co-located with abundant clean power, scaled in standardised 12.5 MW modules. We earn trust through specificity: megawatts, locations, dates, partners.

Logo usage

Keep the mark recognisable across every context.

Do

  • Use on white, navy, or sand backgrounds
  • Maintain clear space of at least 1× cap height
  • Scale proportionally — never stretch or skew
  • Use the full PNG/SVG asset, not a recreation

Don't

  • Recolour the logo or swap palette tiles
  • Place over busy imagery without a backing block
  • Add drop shadows, glows or outlines
  • Rotate, crop, or rearrange the geometric tiles

Photography & imagery

Calm, low-saturation, dramatic light. Cold ambient palettes for site shots — warm sand tones reserved for human moments.

Mood

  • Wide, atmospheric Nordic landscapes
  • Industrial heritage and infrastructure detail
  • Hard, natural light — no studio fill
  • Low-saturation editing; navy / teal / mist
  • People shown working — not posing

Avoid

  • Generic stock data-centre rack shots
  • Glossy CGI server-room renders
  • Saturated sunsets, lens flares, cliché tech
  • Over-edited HDR or heavy filters

Motion

Quiet, purposeful, never decorative. Motion confirms — it does not entertain.

Reveal on scroll

Sections fade in with a 16px upward translate as they enter the viewport. 600ms ease, once-only.

Parallax

Hero decoration moves at 0.15× scroll rate. Background patterns use background-attachment: fixed for a subtle parallax effect.

Reduced motion

All motion respects prefers-reduced-motion. No surprise animations — ever.

Accessibility

Contrast, focus, navigability — non-negotiable.

Contrast

All body and headline copy meets WCAG AA against its background. Test before shipping new pairings.

Focus

Visible focus rings on every interactive element. 2px teal outline at 3px offset.

Skip links

Every page exposes a keyboard-only skip-to-content link as the first focusable element.

Voice & tone

Confident, technical, and grounded. We earn trust through specificity.

We are

Direct. Quantitative. Operator-minded. We lead with capacity, location and economics — not adjectives.

We aren't

Hyperbolic. We avoid "revolutionary," "world-class," "cutting-edge" — the Nordics, the megawatts, and the customers do that work for us.

Numbers, not claims

"1.7 GW pipeline across ten projects" beats "massive portfolio." If a metric exists, lead with it.

Plain English

We use industry terms (CAPEX, OPEX, PPA, GPU/TPU/ASIC) but never jargon for its own sake. Sentences are short.
