Brand guidelines

The Scale42 design system.

A reference for the logo, colour palette, typography, components and tone of voice that define the Scale42 brand across every surface.

Logo

The Scale42 wordmark sits inside a geometric block of brand colours. Use it with generous clear space — never less than the height of the "S".

Logo usage

Keep the mark recognisable across every context.

Do

  • Use on white, navy, or sand backgrounds
  • Maintain clear space of at least 1× cap height
  • Scale proportionally — never stretch or skew
  • Use the full PNG/SVG asset, not a recreation

Don't

  • Recolour the logo or swap palette tiles
  • Place over busy imagery without a backing block
  • Add drop shadows, glows or outlines
  • Rotate, crop, or rearrange the geometric tiles

Voice & tone

Confident, technical, and grounded. We earn trust through specificity.

We are

Direct. Quantitative. Operator-minded. We lead with capacity, location and economics — not adjectives.

We aren't

Hyperbolic. We avoid "revolutionary," "world-class," "cutting-edge" — the Nordics, the megawatts, and the customers do that work for us.

Numbers, not claims

"1.7 GW pipeline across ten projects" beats "massive portfolio." If a metric exists, lead with it.

Plain English

We use industry terms (CAPEX, OPEX, PPA, GPU/TPU/ASIC) but never jargon for its own sake. Sentences are short.
