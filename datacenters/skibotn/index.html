Project description (long)
Identified by the Scale42 team in 2023 as a strong data-centre candidate at the Norwegian landing point of the Far North Fibre project. Backed by both the current landowner and the municipality. Brownfield site with existing industrial buildings; surrounding fjord sparsely populated with natural woodland. Greenfield development possible on areas within the plot.
