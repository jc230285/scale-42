Project description (long)
Brought to Scale42 October 2025 by the municipality. Through stakeholder engagement and capability demos, Scale42 out-bid 5 other developers. Partnership negotiations underway. Large undulating plot mostly native woodland near the Miessaure hydroelectric dam.
