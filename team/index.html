Our journey

From a single rack of servers to a multi-gigawatt pipeline.

  1. 2016

    Initial server testing

    Proof of concept.

  2. 2017

    Production

    Leasing UK data centre space.

  3. 20182 MW

    Hydrokraft AS founded

    First Norwegian operation.

  4. 2019

    Høyanger data centre

    Developed under leasehold and currently held by Scale42.

  5. 202130 MW

    Phase I

    Build-out completed and operational.

  6. 2022

    Sale of Hydrokraft

    $32 m exit, MOIC of 16× in under 4 years and an IRR of 166%. Renamed NScale AS.

  7. 2023

    Scale42 founded

    With assets excluded from the Hydrokraft sale; cap table cleared.

  8. 2024–2025

    Pipeline developed · GIGA-42 JV established

    Planning, power and designs across the Nordic pipeline. GIGA-42 JV established with Global Interconnection Group.

  9. 2026–2030Multi-GW

    AI data centres deployed & scaled

    Multi-GW pipeline developed.
