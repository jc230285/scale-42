Who we are

What this site collects

Information you send us

If you email us or submit an enquiry, we process your name, email and any commercial details you share to respond and to evaluate the opportunity. Lawful basis: legitimate interest (responding to a contact you initiated) or pre-contractual steps. We retain enquiry correspondence for up to 24 months unless a commercial relationship is established.

Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Limited processors are used for hosting and email delivery. We will share data only when required by law or with your consent.

Your rights (GDPR)

You may request access, correction, erasure, restriction, portability or object to processing of your data. Email info@scale-42.com. You may also lodge a complaint with the Norwegian Data Protection Authority (Datatilsynet).

Updates

This policy may be updated as the site evolves. Material changes will be highlighted on the home page.
