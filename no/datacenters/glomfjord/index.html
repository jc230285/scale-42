Location

Nordland, Norway.

Glomfjord is an industrial-heritage location on Norway's northern coast — historically a hydropower og chemicals hub, with permitted grid capacity, deep-water port access og existing transmission infrastructure.

  • Power: Long-duration Norwegian hydropower with strong PPA optionality.
  • Climate: Year-round low ambient — minimal mechanical cooling load.
  • Connectivity: Multiple Nordic fibre routes; sub-30ms RTT to Stockholm/Frankfurt at scale.
  • Permitting: Industrial zoning in place; municipal partnership active.
