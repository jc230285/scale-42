Fibre commentary
One of the strongest-connected sites in the Nordics. Three independent subsea systems land in Iceland (FARICE, DANICE, IRIS) with direct routes to UK, mainland Europe and North America. Systems being renewed and expanded with new/upgraded NA connectivity. Multiple technical and commercial discussions with FARICE via JLL.
