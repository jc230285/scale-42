GIGA-42

Power and compute, jointly venturing.

GIGA-42 is the joint venture between Scale42 and Global Interconnection Group (GIG) — pairing Scale42's data-centre development capability with GIG's energy-sector relationships and capital. One platform, from the substation to the rack.

Scale42

Data centre design, build and operations. Pipeline development, customer-facing engineering.

GIG

Power origination, grid relationships and capital structuring across the Nordics and Europe.

GIGA-42

The integrated vehicle — single counterparty for power-and-compute deals.

What it unlocks

An integrated platform, not a stack of vendors.

One contract for power + compute

Customers procure capacity from a single counterparty rather than negotiating PPAs, grid connections and DC build separately. Faster to signed term sheet, faster to energise.

Capital aligned with the build

GIG's balance sheet underwrites long-lead infrastructure (substations, fibre, civils) so customer capex isn't gating the schedule.

Pan-Nordic optionality

Norway, Iceland, Finland, Sweden — pick the energy mix, latency profile and timeline that fits the workload, not the asset that happens to be available.

Operator DNA on both sides

GIGA-42 is built by people who have run hydropower assets and data centres — not financiers buying the category. Deals are structured to operate, not to flip.
