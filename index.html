Scale42 Pan-Nordic portfolio

The Nordics are the logical location for European AI infrastructure

Scale42, together with its JV GIGA-42, is developing 6,985 MW of AI data centre capacity across four Nordic countries.

Abundant renewable power, structurally lower cost, and stable operating environments create the optimal conditions for large-scale AI infrastructure.

Our sites are positioned for strong fibre connectivity, near-term power availability, and integration with heat reuse applications.
